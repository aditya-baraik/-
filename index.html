Introduction

About Me

Computer Instructor and English Honours student with a deep interest in creative technology, visual storytelling, and digital experimentation.

My work lives at the intersection of design, photography, writing, and emerging AI tools — always exploring new ways to transform ideas into visual and digital experiences.

I've built hands-on experience in graphic design, poster and magazine-style layouts, photography, and photo editing — using Canva, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe Lightroom. I also experiment with AI-powered creative tools including image generation and generative fill.

Alongside visual work, I explore the technical side — HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python, building websites, Telegram bots, and Android widgets with AI assistance. I also tinker with Arduino and Raspberry Pi for DIY electronics projects. As a Computer Instructor, I've trained 60+ students in basic computing and MS Office.

Beyond all of it, I write poetry and short stories — exploring themes of life and human emotion. Storytelling in every form it takes.
